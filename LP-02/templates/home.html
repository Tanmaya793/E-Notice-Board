Dear User, Welcome to the University Notice Board!

The University Notice Board is an easy-to-use platform designed to keep everyone in the university community informed.

This system allows for seamless communication between different user levels, such as:

• Students: Receive important updates and announcements.

• Teachers: Share notices with students and colleagues.

• HODs: Send department-related notices to teachers and students.

• Deans: Issue campus-wide notices to all levels.

To begin with University Notice Board, start by creating your profile. Keep the notices professional & be respectful to everyone.
