University Notice Board

The University Notice Board Project is designed to provide a centralized platform for sharing important announcements, events, and updates with students, faculty, and staff. The main objective of this project is to replace traditional notice boards with a more efficient, accessible, and eco-friendly system that can be updated in real time.

*User-friendly interface
*Role Based Access
*To reduce paper Waste
*To Provide centralized platform
